# Bare-Handed

## About This Column

Bare-Handed is a daily training log. Each entry starts from a blank page, without books or notes, to confront a single physics concept or derivation. The goal is not to produce polished exposition, but to expose gaps, diagnose blind spots, and rebuild from scratch. What appears here is the raw material of understanding — incomplete by design.
