## Paper #1: Josephson Current and Noise at a Superconductor-Quantum Spin Hall Insulator-Superconductor Junction

**Authors**: Liang Fu, C.L. Kane  
**arXiv**: [0804.4469](https://arxiv.org/abs/0804.4469)  
**Domain**: quantum spin hall, majorana, josephson, superconductivity, topology, proximity  
**Year**: 2008

---

**Summary**: 
Seminal work exploring the interplay between topological quantum spin Hall insulators and superconductivity. The paper theoretically investigates Josephson junctions formed by superconductors on either side of a 2D topological insulator (quantum spin Hall insulator). Key predictions include zero-energy Majorana bound states manifesting as fractional AC Josephson effect (4π-periodic), and noise spectrum calculations to distinguish Majorana modes from conventional Andreev bound states.

**Why collected**: 
Foundational paper connecting topological insulators and superconductivity with clear experimental predictions. Established theoretical basis for creating and detecting Majorana zero modes in superconductor-topological insulator heterostructures.
